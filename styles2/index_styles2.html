H2 - About Set 2

This first set takes influence from our industry. Both the typography and colour palette are take direction from the federal institutes we work with. The colour palette is made up of red, white, and blue while the typography is based closely off USWDS pairings: A serif close to USWDS's Merriweather and sans serif that matches USWDS's Source Sans. The Roboto Slab/Lato combo, however, is a bit heavier than Merriweather/Source Sans to match the bold colours of the colour palette.
