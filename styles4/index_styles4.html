H2 - About Set 4

Set 4 is based of the concept of a honeycomb that CivicActions and TomorrowPartners brainstormed two years ago. Warm colours that are reminiscent of honey and bees make up the colour palette. Ubuntu as a headline type is easy to read, and like a honeycomb, has unique geometry that complements the CivicActions logo. Ubuntu's curves keep it from looking too ridgid, and plays well with the rounded body type, Didact Gothic.
